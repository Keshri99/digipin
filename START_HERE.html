üéÆ How to Use

Step 1: Open a Visualization

Choose from three options above or use this quick start:

Click ‚Üí digipin-visualization.html (Basic) or digipin-advanced.html (Advanced)

Step 2: Explore the Map

You'll see India with 16 grid sectors labeled with characters (F, C, 9, 8, etc.)

  • Hover over sectors - they highlight
  • Click any sector to zoom in
  • Watch coordinates update in real-time

Step 3: Navigate

Use the sidebar controls to navigate:

  • "Go Up" - Navigate to parent level
  • "Reset View" - Return to full India
  • Click sectors - Jump to specific areas

Step 4: Use Tools (Advanced Dashboard)

Click the Tools tab to:

  • Encode coordinates to DigiPin
  • Decode DigiPin to coordinates
  • Search for specific locations
